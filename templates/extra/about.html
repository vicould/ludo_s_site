About

It seems you are trying to learn more about me, well, let's go. Hello I am Ludovic Delaveau !

For the moment I am still student in computer science in the École des Mines de Saint-Étienne, right here : it's in France, not far from Lyon in case it might interest you.

As you might have seen I am kind of a geek, interested by simple and good code. Code which might be in Python, Objective-C, as well as Java (learned at school, and used in an internship). I have been system and network administrator in my hall of residence during one year, and I gained there a lot of experience I will try to share. I was responsible with Benjamin Loulier (see blog roll) for ten servers under various flavors of Unix, and for more than three hundred students.

Apart from these geeky activities, I am rather interested in design (it was my minor during grad school). I also like photography (come and see my flickr, until I make a gallery also here) and architecture. I enjoy riding mountain bike when I am home (it's near Grenoble, there), as well as hiking, climbing and skiing.
